Business Goal Demo Recommendation
- Safest demo query: Q2 Peak Sales Hours Analysis
- Why this is a good choice:
  - easy to explain in plain English
  - clearly tied to a Phase 1 business goal
  - has a clean tabular output
  - easy to visualize as a bar chart
  - visibly changes after the update script

Recommended Business Demo Structure
1. English business question
   - What hours of the day are busiest for customer orders, and how much revenue is generated during each hour?
2. SQL query
   - Use Q2 from projectDBqueries.sql
3. Query output table
   - Show the before-update result
   - Then show the after-update result to prove updates affect the analytics
4. Visualization
   - Use a bar chart with sale_hour on the x-axis and either num_orders or hour_revenue on the y-axis
   - Best talking point: hour 12 becomes the clear lunch rush after inserting clustered noon orders

Good Chart Choices
- Q1 Top-Selling Products: horizontal bar chart of total_units_sold by product_name
- Q2 Peak Sales Hours: bar chart of num_orders by sale_hour
- Q4 Revenue by Category/Product: stacked bar chart or subtotal table, but this is harder to present quickly
- Q5 Delivery Cost by Supplier/Month: grouped bar chart, but this is also harder to explain live
